# Probability Rules

## Probability Formula

P(A) = Number of favorable outcomes / Total outcomes

---

## Properties

0 ≤ P(A) ≤ 1

P(S) = 1

P(∅) = 0

---

## Complement Rule

P(A') = 1 - P(A)

---

## Addition Rule

P(A ∪ B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A ∩ B)

---

## Independent Events

P(A ∩ B) = P(A) × P(B)

---

## Dice Example

Two dice are thrown.

Total outcomes:

6 × 6 = 36

Example:

Sum = 8

Possible outcomes:

(2,6)  
(3,5)  
(4,4)  
(5,3)  
(6,2)

Total favorable = 5

Probability:

P = 5/36

---
